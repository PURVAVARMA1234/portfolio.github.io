Computer Aided Design(FUSION-360)

Computer-Aided Design (CAD) is the use of computers in the design process across various industries. It allows designers, engineers, and architects to create precise 2D drawings

Fusion 360 is like a digital workshop where you can design and create 3D models of pretty much anything you can imagine, from gadgets to buildings. It's all done on your computer, and you can tweak and change your designs easily. Plus, it lets you simulate how your designs will work in the real world and even helps you make them with machines like 3D printers or CNC routers. So, whether you're a hobbyist, student, or professional, Fusion 360 is a handy tool for bringing your ideas to life.
